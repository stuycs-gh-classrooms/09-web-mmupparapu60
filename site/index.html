This website is all about Ski Racing!! Ski racing is a competitive style of skiing where people ski down a course and race to have to fastest time. The course is made up of gates with twwo different colors, usually blue and red. You have to go around the outside of the gates to finish the course. The distance betwween the gates and the general speed of the course is dependant on the event.
