Giant Slalom

All about GS!!

This is the second event. This event is faster than slalom but slower than super G. Instead of a single pole, the gates in GS are twwo poles with a red or blue panel or shirt. The turns in GS are a widder and more spaced out, so there is more time in between turns. In GS people use longer skies and don't always wear guards. Giant Slalom info
